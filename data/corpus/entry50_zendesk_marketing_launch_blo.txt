Frequently asked questions
What is Zendesk AI?
Zendesk AI is the intelligence powering our Resolution Platform. Zendesk AI enhances every part of the service experience by providing AI-powered tools for agents and admins and automating customer interactions with AI agents. Zendesk offers a set of AI-powered tools in the Suite, as well as a robust set of tools for agents and admins in the Copilot add-on.
What are AI agents in customer service?
AI agents are autonomous systems designed to understand and autonomously resolve complex issues on any channel. Unlike traditional chatbots, they can reason across multi-step requests, execute actions across business systems and continuously improve based on outcomes. Learn more about Zendesk AI agents
here
.
How does Zendesk AI improve ticketing?
AI ticketing automation provides advantages over traditional ticketing systems. It automates tasks so businesses can do more with less, boosting efficiency and reducing costs. AI improves
customer satisfaction
through fast response times, quick ticket resolution and personalised support. It also improves over time by learning from each interaction. Learn more about the Zendesk AI-powered ticketing system
here
.
How can I access Zendesk AI solutions?
Zendesk currently provides some AI features within our Suite, including generative search. For more information on pricing and packaging,
view our pricing page
.
How is Zendesk protecting my data?
Zendesk AI is built on the core principles of privacy, security and compliance, by design. Zendesk leverages a set of
design principles
that not only set the standard for how we design, develop and build everything we do, but that set a clear foundation for our use of AI for customer experiences (CX) and employee experience (EX). Learn more about our approach
here
.
How does Zendesk work with OpenAI and other Large Language Models?
Zendesk AI leverages a flexible, modular architecture that enables the use of multiple leading Large Language Models (LLMs), including frontier and open-source models. Generative AI features in Zendesk are currently powered by OpenAI (using zero data retention endpoints) or models hosted on Microsoft Azure, Amazon Bedrock or Google Cloud Platform (where the model provider never has access to prompts or outputs). Learn more about our multi-LLM architecture
here
.
