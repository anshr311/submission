Yes.

I am a developer or a company who wants to create a new application based on Falcon 180B (for example a chatbot) and then offer it as a paid service accessible through either a dedicated platform or an API. Is this allowed in the license?
Yes.

I work for a major corporation. We plan to utilize Falcon 180B in our internal tools. Is this allowed under the license?
Yes.
The license allows companies and developers to use Falcon 180B within their internal tools and applications, and host it in their own or leased infrastructure.
I am a hosting provider and I do provide dedicated infrastructure for developers or companies willing to host their dedicated instances of LLM models including Falcon 180B. Is this allowed under the license?
Yes.

The license only restricts the provisioning of shared instances offered as an inference / finetuning API (subject to another license)
I am a hosting provider and I want to host instances of Falcon 180B (base model or fine-tuned versions of the model) as a standalone shared service. Can I charge access to it through an API (inference API or finetuning API)?
No.
