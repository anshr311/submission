Galaxy AI.
A simple way to make life more seamless
Galaxy AI is your true AI companion that comes integrated into Samsung Galaxy phones, tablets and wearables. It helps you navigate your world with ease and confidence, whether it's for creativity, personalization, communication, productivity or health. By understanding what you need and turning it into action, Galaxy AI brings practical help into your everyday life and helps you focus simply on what you want to accomplish.
A photo of a dog is seen on the screen of a Galaxy S26 Ultra phone. Galaxy AI icon is selected in the Gallery app. In Photo Assist, the user can describe what to change by inputting a text prompt. After typing 'Make this his birthday party', the Generate button is pressed. Galaxy AI transforms the photo by adding various birthday themed accessories on and around the dog. Options to Show previous, Save copy and Keep editing for the photo appear on the screen. The Show previous button is pressed, causing the previous unedited picture of the dog to appear next to the phone for comparison, along with the text prompt to 'Make this his birthday party'. Can your phone do that?
Already here and running, Galaxy AI offers a wide variety of features that you can jump right into. Get your Galaxy device and try out new ways to create and communicate, to plan and understand, to get things done and get ahead.

Photo Assist
Make your picture perfect in seconds
Photo Assist on Galaxy phones and tablets makes editing your photos effortless. Move, resize or delete objects by hand and have AI fill in the gaps for a seamless look. You can also enter a prompt or reference image to add new elements into the image, like putting a hat on a subject. Enjoy the freedom to imagine, the room to be creative and the fun of trying out different looks.1
Creative Studio
Create the unique designs of your imagination
Explore your artistic side in the Creative Studio. Create your own personalized designs for greeting cards, wallpapers and more by combining text with drawings or images. Generate sticker sets in the styles and concepts you want, like cute emojis to show your friends. There's just so much to explore, imagine and share.2
Audio Eraser
Filter out the noise and crank up the vibes
Audio Eraser removes background noise from videos in a couple of taps, cleaning up your audio like a charm. Use it across Galaxy phones and tablets to easily edit the moments you've filmed in your life, or use it to clear up distractions from videos shared on certain apps. From the concerts you've attended to content shared by others, you can hear every second in crystal clarity — especially while listening with your Galaxy Buds.3
Auto Trim
Instantly compile your best moments
Want a handy highlight reel of what you did this weekend? Auto Trim can make one for you instantly, extracting key moments in your videos to create a clip of highlights centered around a specific activity. You'll get beautifully edited videos to share with your friends and family without the work of actual editing.4

How Galaxy AI keeps your data secure
Enjoy peace of mind with Samsung's security, designed for the era of AI. We use multiple safeguards to ensure that your personal details stay personal.
Turn your device into a digital fortress
Rest assured when it comes to on device protection for your Galaxy. Our Personal Data Engine gathers and processes your personal data safely. Then it encrypts that data and saves it onto your device with KEEP and Knox Vault, making it harder for anyone else to gain access.15
Take control of your privacy settings
You can decide where your Galaxy AI data gets processed — either locally on device or out in the cloud. Go to your privacy settings and choose the option that gives you the most assurance, or switch back and forth depending on your needs.
Establish trust across your Galaxy
Your connected devices become safer when they look out for each other. Our Knox Matrix Trust Chain technology provides cross-platform security, allowing Galaxy devices to check on one another and isolate potential threats.16

What devices have Galaxy AI?
Galaxy AI comes built into Samsung devices like smartphones, smartwatches, earbuds and tablets, although the available features vary by device.
⦁ Galaxy AI is supported on smartphones starting from the S24 Series phones onward.
⦁ Galaxy AI is supported on tablets and wearable products (such as Galaxy Watch and Galaxy Buds), but the level of support depends on the model and OS version.
⦁ The availability of certain Galaxy AI features may vary by region as well as model. If you want to be sure of getting a certain Galaxy AI feature, check beforehand that it's provided on that particular model in your region.
⦁ Please note that the supported features and models may change with future product releases and updates.
